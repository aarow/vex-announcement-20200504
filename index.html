About

Vex Yoga offers online and private yoga classes designed to fit your busy lifestyle. Yoga sequences designed to provide you with the perfect combination physical movement, recovery, and mindfulness in your own home at an affordable cost. Our mission is to create a space where everybody is comfortable, from beginners through advanced students.
